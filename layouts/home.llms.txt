# Viktor Petersson

> {{ .Site.Params.description }}

Viktor Petersson is a tech entrepreneur, security advocate, and host of the "Nerding Out with Viktor" podcast. He is based in London and has been building and running tech companies for over 15 years. He leads Screenly, the world's first developer-friendly digital signage platform powering over 10,000 screens globally.
